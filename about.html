About Me

Born and raised in La Union, Philippines, my journey began in college, where I first stepped into the spotlight through pageantry in 2024. What started as a personal challenge quickly became a passion, leading to multiple titles and growing recognition. These experiences built my confidence in front of the camera and on stage, ultimately opening the doors to the commercial modeling world.

Now, I’m focused on bringing brands to life—whether through print, digital campaigns, or live events. I pride myself on being adaptable, expressive, and easy to work with, always showing up prepared and professional. My background in pageantry has trained me to carry poise under pressure, while my love for visual storytelling keeps me authentic and engaging in every frame.
